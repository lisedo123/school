About Our Project

Our journey began in Fall of 2023

Our app, Back2School, is like a magical store for teachers and parents. It is a place to find school supplies without breaking the bank. We understand that buying pencils, books, and other things for school can sometimes be expensive. But we believe that every student deserves the chance to have the tools they need for a great education.
